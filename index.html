Brains for Brainless People

(Adam Elzarka)

Founders: Jared Stegman, Anuj Mantha, and Jake Louis

Our invention is a brain. We started selling these 2 months ago and we have sold 20 million so far. People who buy these are mainly hospitals and Adams'. We have 5 main warehouses that produce these. Across the world we have 100,000 stores as well as an online store. Our main warehouse is in right here in Cincinnati, Ohio. It costs us about one thousand dollars to make one. We sell the brains for 500,000 dollars each plus shipping and handling.We are asking for 999 trillion dollars for half of our company. With the 999 trillion dollars we will build more warehouses and to find more effecient ways to make the brains. For other Adams'. The brain is the most important organ in the entire human body, sadly Adam has no important organ in his entire body. The brain is made up of many specialized areas that work together, for example, the brain tells the heart to pump. Now you really wonder how Adam is still alive. Of course, Adam Elzarka is the most consuming customer because he can't find a way to use a brain. But we can't make Adam feel bad, so that's why he's not judging our product.
